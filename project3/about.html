The website is designed as a digital sanctuary for college students in New York, offering them a much-needed breather from academic stress through thoughtfully curated getaway ideas. Centered around the concept of “touching grass,” the site invites users to explore personalized trips within, around, and outside the city—from tranquil corners in local parks and hidden coffee spots to scenic day trips and nature escapes just a train ride away. With its calming visuals and intuitive navigation, the platform encourages students to disconnect from their screens and reconnect with themselves, their surroundings, and a slower pace of life—reminding them that rest is not a luxury, but a vital part of well-being.
