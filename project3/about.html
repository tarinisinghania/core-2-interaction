The website is designed as a digital sanctuary for college students in New York, offering them a much-needed breather from academic stress through thoughtfully curated getaway ideas.

"Touch grass" is a popular internet slang term meaning to spend time outdoors and disconnect from online activities. This site invites users to explore personalized trips within, around, and outside the city—from tranquil corners in local parks and hidden fun spots to scenic day trips and nature escapes just a train ride away. With its calming visuals and intuitive navigation, the platform encourages students to disconnect from their screens and reconnect with themselves, their surroundings, and a slower pace of life—reminding them that rest is not a luxury, but a vital part of well-being.
