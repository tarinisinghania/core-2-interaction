POPULATION

The 2025 Maha Kumbh Mela has already witnessed a staggering 148 million attendees.
Pilgrims from across India and the globe have flocked to Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh, to take part in this sacred festival, which is held every 12 years.
The overwhelming influx of devotees has placed immense pressure on infrastructure, transportation, and security, requiring authorities to undertake massive logistical operations.
The philanthropist and wife of Steve Jobs attended the Kumbh with a large entourage. Chris Martin, lead vocalist of Coldplay, and actress Dakota Johnson made brief visits to the Maha Kumbh Mela, highlighting its global appeal.
